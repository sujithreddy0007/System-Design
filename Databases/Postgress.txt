PostgreSQL
Definition

PostgreSQL is an open-source relational database management system (RDBMS) known for its ACID compliance, advanced SQL features, extensibility, and reliability.

Characteristics
Relational database
ACID compliant
Strong consistency
Advanced SQL support
Extensible and highly customizable
Why Use PostgreSQL?
Complex queries
Advanced indexing
Strong data integrity
Supports JSON in addition to relational data
